VA task — research
Research and summarise information
Your client wants to know about 3 project management tools to potentially use for their team of 5. Without looking anything up (use what you know), summarise Trello, Asana, and Notion as if you were a VA presenting options to your client. Keep it brief and professional.
Pro tip: A VA research summary has: tool name, what it does in one sentence, best for (team size/use case), pricing tier, and a recommendation. Never copy-paste — always rewrite in your own words.

Advanced — VA client brief
Respond to a real client brief
A new client sent you this message. Respond professionally as their VA — acknowledge the tasks, ask any clarifying questions you need, and confirm your understanding of what you'll deliver.
Hi! So happy to have you on board. Quick rundown of what I need this week: - Sort out my emails (there's like 200+) - Book flights to Cape Town for me and my colleague for next Thursday - Find a good accountant in JHB - Put together a weekly report template I can use going forward - Also can u chase the guys at BrandCo they haven't responded to our quote Let me know what you need from me. Thanks!
Pro tip: A great VA response organises tasks by priority, flags what info is missing, confirms deadlines, and sounds confident — not overwhelmed.
